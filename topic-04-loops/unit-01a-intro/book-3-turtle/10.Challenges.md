# Challenges

## Challenge 1 – Shape Generator

Ask the user for:

- number of sides
- side length
- colour

Draw the requested shape.

## Challenge 2 – Flower Garden

Create a function:

~~~python
draw_flower(t, x, y, colour)
~~~

Use a loop to draw several flowers in different positions.

## Challenge 3 – City Skyline

Use loops and functions to draw buildings.

Each building should have:

- a rectangle body
- windows
- a roof or antenna

## Challenge 4 – Fireworks

Use loops to draw lines radiating from a central point.

Hint:

~~~python
for ray in range(36):
    t.forward(100)
    t.backward(100)
    t.left(10)
~~~

## Challenge 5 – Pattern Wall

Create a repeated tile pattern using squares, triangles or circles.

## Challenge 6 – Turtle Art Competition

Create a piece of turtle art that includes:

- nested loops
- functions
- at least five colours
- clear repeated patterns
