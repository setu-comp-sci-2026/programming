# 8. Final Design Challenge

Create your own turtle artwork using loops and functions.

Your design must include:

- at least one `for` loop
- at least one function
- at least three colours
- at least two different shapes
- a repeated pattern

## Possible Ideas

- flower garden
- city skyline
- robot face
- colourful spiral
- village of houses
- birthday card
- fireworks
- maze pattern

## Planning

Before coding, write down:

1. What are you drawing?
2. What shapes are repeated?
3. Which parts could be functions?
4. Which parts need loops?
